CONTROLS

  • Left Arrow: Move left and face left
  • Right Arrow: Move right and face right

The following apply in the direction your character is facing.

  • Down Arrow: Pick up or drop block
  • Up Arrow: Move upwards (climb on block)

Other functionality

  • r: Reset level
  • shift+arrows: Pan level

Mobile controls

Use the floating buttons to control the fly.

Drag your finger on the level to pan it.
